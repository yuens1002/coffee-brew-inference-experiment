    Under the hood

    Community consensus, not machine guessing

    People log their brews

    Every logged brew — origin, roast, method, grind, temp, ratio, and rating — goes into the community knowledge base.

    Weighted consensus scoring

    When you ask for a recommendation, Brew Guide matches your origin, roast, and method against logged data — favouring higher-rated entries. Confidence tiers (high / medium / low) tell you how much data backs the result.

    Method-specific technique

    Each of 8 brewing methods has its own technique schema: bloom timing, pour stages, steep times, agitation. The relevant technique surfaces with your recommendation.

    MCP endpoint for any AI

    Everything is exposed over the Model Context Protocol. Claude, Cursor, Hermes — any MCP-capable agent can query Brew Guide mid-conversation, no config beyond one URL.

    Connect your AI assistant

    One line. Any AI. Every brew question answered.

    Brew Guide exposes a public MCP endpoint — no auth required. Connect it once and any agent you use can answer brew questions from your community's real experience.
